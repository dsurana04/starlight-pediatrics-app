⭐ Orion — $295/mo (0–2 months)

Just like the mighty hunter Orion, this plan provides strength and protection for your baby's earliest days.

  • Expectant parent meet-and-greet
  • Newborn preparation, education, and support
  • 3 in-home newborn visits
  • Newborn sleep and lactation support
  • Sick or problem-based visits
  • All well-visits and checkups
  • Immediate text responses — no voicemails
  • Vaccination access via pharmacy partnership

🎶 Lyra — $225/mo (3–12 months)

Lyra, the harp, represents harmony and growth during this precious first year.

  • Well-visits and checkups
  • Sick or problem-based visits
  • Developmental monitoring and guidance
  • Office-based visits
  • Lactation support and feeding help
  • Vaccination access via pharmacy partnership

⭐ Sirius — $175/mo (1–4 years)

Sirius, the brightest star, lights the way through the toddler years of rapid growth.

  • Wellness visits and checkups
  • Sick or problem-based visits
  • Office-based visits
  • Developmental screening and guidance
  • Nutrition, sleep, and behavior guidance
  • Vaccination access via pharmacy partnership

🦄 Pegasus — $150/mo (5–18 years)

Inspired by Pegasus, the winged horse, fostering strength, imagination, and freedom.

  • Wellness visits and checkups
  • Sick or problem-based visits
  • Office-based visits
  • Nutrition, sleep, and behavior guidance + developmental screening
  • Sports physicals
  • Routine anxiety/depression screening (adolescents)
  • Vaccination access via pharmacy partnership

⭐ Polaris — $100/mo (19–22 years)

Much like Polaris, the North Star, providing stability and direction during life's transitions.

  • Remote care coordination (text, email, video)
  • Prescription management
  • Continued health guidance — avoid student health center hassles

💰 Family Savings

  • 25% discount for additional children
  • Family maximum: $500/month
  • 5% discount for annual prepayment
  • $100 one-time enrollment fee per family
  • $50 travel fee for in-home visits (families with children 3mo+)

Plan Transition Rules (EMR)

Orion → Lyra: When baby turns 3 months, reduce from $295 to $225. Change in EMR effective the 1st of the month after they turn 3 months.

Lyra → Sirius: When child turns 1 year, reduce from $225 to $175. Change in EMR effective the 1st of the month after they turn 1 year.

Sirius → Pegasus: When child turns 4 years, reduce from $175 to $150. Change in EMR effective the 1st of the month after they turn 4 years.

Pegasus → Polaris: When young adult turns 19, reduce from $150 to $100. Change in EMR effective the 1st of the month after they turn 19.

Tip: The app automatically calculates these dates for each patient and alerts you on the Actions page.

How Your Data Works

✅
Closing the browser: Your data is safe. It stays in your browser's storage.
✅
Restarting your computer: Your data is safe. Same browser = same data.
⚠️
Clearing browser data: This WILL delete your data. Restore from a backup file if this happens.
⚠️
Different device/browser: Each browser has its own copy. Use backup/restore to transfer.
💡
Best practice: Download a backup every time you add or change patients. It takes 2 seconds.
